Online Placement Examination

Instructions

  1. Only full-screen mode is available for students to give tests.
  2. If a student attempts to reduce the screen, a message will appear stating that the student has only 10 seconds to return to full-screen mode before the test is auto-submitted and the result page is presented.
  3. If a student refreshes the screen by accident, his progress is not lost. The progress will be saved, and the timer will be reset to where it was before being refreshed.
  4. If a student tries to switch tabs, desktops, or browsers during the test, a warning will appear twice, indicating that the screen has been changed. If the student continues to try to move the tab despite multiple warnings, his test will be auto-submitted.
  5. A student cannot take more than one test at the same time. If he tries to open the same test in a different tab, he will be instantly logged out of the one he just closed.
