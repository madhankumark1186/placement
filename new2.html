Educational institutions have evolved over the years, thanks to technological advancements. With smart classrooms and Learning Management Systems (LMS), it is now easier for teachers to conduct interactive learning sessions, map students' knowledge, and create progress reports. These advancements make it easier for students to learn efficiently and for teachers to evaluate students with less effort and in less time.

What is an online examination system?

An online examination system is a computer-based test system that can be used to conduct computer-based tests online. This examination system uses fewer resources and reduces the need for question papers and answer scripts, exam room scheduling, arranging invigilators, coordinating with examiners, and more.

How to conduct online examination?

To administer a secure online examination, begin by choosing the type of online test you want to deliver, map the candidates who must appear for the examination, and send out email notifications with test details to the candidates. The candidates can click on the link provided in the email or login with their details and appear for the exam online.
